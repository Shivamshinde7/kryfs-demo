Introducing BOLA

"To prevent transformer failures and increase the life and reliability of transformers, KRYFS Technologies has developed a low-cost solution, named BOLA (Better Operations and Life by Analytics) which is an IOT remote monitoring solution for Distribution, Power Transformers, Electrical machines, and Energy Meters. BOLA will monitor the vital parameters of a transformer (Electrical and physical) in real-time from any location on our proprietary dashboard on your mobile and computer. The solution diagnoses & sends alerts/notifications on SMS & email whenever there is any abnormality sensed and hence allows to take preventive action before the actual failure happens. It can be retrofitted on any old or new Transformer. The risk of failure is eliminated using alarm rules and detections of deviations for the reference values of threshold (E.g. Oil and winding temperature, Transformer loading, and Oil level)
